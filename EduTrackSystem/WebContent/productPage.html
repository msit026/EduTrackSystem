EduTrackSystem

Edu-track-system is a web application designed by our team for education institutions to manage student records and reports. It is designed to provide a flexible approach for schools and universities to generate student reports.

It handles the functions like:

  • Handling records of examinations, assessments, marks, grades and academic progression
  • Maintaining records of attendance and courses
  • Providing statistical reports with Data Analysis, Performance, Charts, CGPA tool and Feedback Mechanism included.
